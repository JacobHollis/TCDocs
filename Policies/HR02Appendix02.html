# HR02: Appendix 2 - Recruitment and Selection Process Summary

Appended to:

# HR02 - Recruitment

# Recruitment

  • Vacancy agreed by Operations Director
  • JD and Job Spec reviewed within week 1
  • Advert agreed within week 1
  • Advertised within week 2
  • Shortlisting held within week 4
  • Interviews held within week 6
  • offer made on the day if possible and within 5 working days of interview maximum
  • Complete employment checks within 1 week of interview
  • Complete and sign off contract within 1 week of interviews

# Selection

  • Agree selection scoring methodology
  • Consider if a workshop required if need to reduce shortlist from more than 4 people
  • Organise workshop to include experts by experience
  • Shortlist no more than 4 people to progress to inteview
  • Interview panel to include experts by experience
  • Agree with panel the format of inteview:
    • practical assessment
    • presentation
    • interview
  • Agree the scoring of each phase
  • Undertake the interview
  • Panel decision made
  • Chair of panel agrees to inform candidiates of outcomes

# Offer and Contract Within 1 Week

  • Contact those unsucessful and provide feedback
  • Make offer subject to employment and occupational health checks
  • Check references
  • Check Occupational Health and identify if any outstanding or ongoing health interventions required
  • Check DBS and ensure clearance
  • Check I.D. and UK rights to work status
  • Check references
  • Draft contract written - agree start date, annual leave, etc
  • Operations Director sign off and completes contract
  • Contract includes start T&Cs
  • Contract sent to candiadte for them to sign and return
