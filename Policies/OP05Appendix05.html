# OP05: Appendix 5 - Information Sharing Consent Form

Appended to:

# OP05 - Programme Development

# Information Sharing Consent Form (Download Link)

  • All programmes should have a completed Information Sharing Consent Form and this should be reviewed on a regular basis.

  • This form is to clarify with the person that we will need to share information on an ‘needs to know’ basis but assure them that we will maintain their confidentiality at all times unless there is a concern which needs information to be shared.

  • The form may need to be completed by a member of their family if they lack capacity to sign initially; this would need to be highlighted on their capacity assessment form.

If the person’s capacity changes and they can consent; this must be recorded and a new form should be prepared for them to sign.
