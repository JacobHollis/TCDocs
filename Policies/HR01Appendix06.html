# HR01: Appendix 6 - Grievance, Bullying and Harassment Procedure Summary

Appended to:

# HR01 - Employment

Our Grievance, Bullying and Harassment Procedure is formed of three steps; Report, Respond & React:

# Report

  • Report any concerns to your line manager or a HR adviser
  • Report to your line manager's manager if it is either about your manager or you are unable / do not want to tell them for some other reason.
  • Agree next steps

# Respond

  • A confidential meeting with you will be held to ask you about your concerns and counselling offered if you would like within 48 hours of reporting. You will be able to bring a supporter with you

  • A confidential meeting with the person you have concerns about will be held by a senior manager and or HR adviser within 48 ours of your meeting with them. They will be able to bring a supporter with them

  • Any witnesses named will also be interviewed to find out what they think has happened.

  • A report will be completed to be presented to the Operations Director to decide next steps - this may include finding out more details or clarifying a point or it will result in a decision on whether there is a case to answer or not

# React

When all the facts are clarified the following reactions may be confirmed:

  • Some evidence of unintended negative behaviour - Person to meet with reported person for mediation

  • Person has behaved in a unfair, offensive, bullying or harrassing way - disciplinary procedure followed.

  • No evidence of wrongdoing - case closed with follow up with both people to ensure they have support to consider the outcome and any actions they wish to happen next
