# OP09: Appendix 2 - Standard hospital checks

Appended to:

# OP09 - Physical Healthcare

Baseline for all Then reviews
U&Es (urea and electrolytes ) Every 6 months
LFTs (Liver Function Test) Annually
FBC (Full Blood Count) Annually
TFTs (TSH and T4) (Thyroid function tests) Annually
Full Lipid profile, Cholesterol, HDL/LDL ratio,(high density lipoprotein and low density lipoproteinâ€“ thyroid function tests), triglycerides Repeat after 3 months then yearly
Random blood sugars Annually
ECG (electrocardiogram ) As clinically indicated; minimum 6 monthly
Weight/BMI (Body Mass Index) As clinically needed; minimum 3 monthly
Blood pressure/pulse Every 6 months or more often if clinically indicated
Prolactin (hormone) test As clinically indicated

Whilst these checks are expected for people entering a hospital environment, the people we support may have physical health needs that require regular and ongoing monitoring of their health via some or all of the checks above.
